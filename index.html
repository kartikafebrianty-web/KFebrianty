Cats are my favourite animals

Cats are a popular pet. Many people have them as a companion friend for their house. They are playful, love to be cuddled and very independent at the same time. In our home, we have a cat that we were adopted since she was a kitten. She is a black and white cat. She loves to play with a furry little ball. We love spending time with her, although her favourite thing is sleeping under a blanket all day.

Things I Love About Cats

  • They are playful and full of energy.
  • They bring comforts for people.
  • They are easy to look after
  • They have unique personalities.
  • They are graceful and elegant.
